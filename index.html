Antonin Maudry
About
Antonin Maudry is a swiss Graphic Designer graduated from ECAL in 2023, currently living and working in Vevey. He is working as a freelance Designer, and together with Thomas K. Neyroud for their project "444 Squadra". Mostly working with type and layouts, Antonin also has a strong image background as a photographer, publishing his pictures in international Skiing and Snowboarding Magazines and doing commissionned work. He is also starting to explore the fields of arts, together with Marjolaine Rey and their project "Réunion".

Contact at anto@444squadra.ch.
Coemeter
Coemeter offers a reinterpretation of the Trajan typeface. This typographic work is showcased on a website. A digital cemetery awaits, where you can engrave your tombstone and personalize your mourning space.

Coemeter plays with dualities, between the sacred and the profane, history and anachronism, matter and consciousness. This unpretentious and poetic experience invites us to reconsider our connection to stone, memory, and loss.
La Fièvre
I worked as a communication manager for the association La Fièvre for one year. I developed the skatepark's identity and handled various communications for its events. I also covered their events as a photographer. Custom font 444 Ella in use.
555 Chrysalide
Self initiated project by Thomas K. Neyroud, Antonin Maudry and Louis Roh in 2021.
555 Chrysalide upcycles clothing selected in thrift stores, giving them a second life. We printed everything by hand in Lausanne.
444 Fonts
Fonts mostly designed during our Bachelor at ECAL, during the course with Aurèle Sack. Most fonts are WIP, some of which we pushed forward for other projects or in personal work.
Buldoz Invitational
2023, Communication for the Buldoz Invitational, An international freeskiing event celebrating the ski community in Switzerland. Project started at ECAL, under the teaching of Diego Bontognali and Nicole Udry.
Paisajes Poéticos de Chile
Project made for a contest organized by ECAL, The swiss Ambassy in Chile and the exposition space Suizspacio. Winner of the contest held in the course with Diego Bontognali, in 2022. Edited and printed in Santiago de Chile, in 2022. Presented at It's a Book in Leipzig.
Suizspacio Posters
These posters were made at ECAL in early 2022, with supervision of Diego Bontognali & Jonas Wandeler, for a contest adressed by the swiss embassy in Chile. The proposals were selected with ten others to be displayed permanently at the Suizpacio, the swiss cultural space, in Santiago de Chile.
Agora Magazine, Vol. 1
Agora is a magazine curated and designed by Antonin Maudry, Laeticia Schwendi and Laeticia Jakob under the supervision of Anouk Schneider and Chi-Long Trieu. This magazine is an hommage to collaboration, our process being meeting and linking different creators.
7–10 Figo
7-10 Figo retraces the biggest transfer in football of the 2000s. This event takes the form of a newspaper with a double print, symbolizing the snake's pit that governed this event.
